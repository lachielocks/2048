How to Play

Use your arrow keys or WASD to move all tiles. Matching numbers merge into one when they collide. Keep combining to reach 2048 and beyond.

Special rules for bigger grids

  • 4×4: 1 new tile appears after each move.
  • 5×5: 2 new tiles appear after each move.
  • 6×6: 3 new tiles appear after each move.
  • 7×7: 4 new tiles appear after each move, and merge score is reduced to 50%.
  • 8×8: 5 new tiles appear after each move, and merge score is reduced to 50%.
